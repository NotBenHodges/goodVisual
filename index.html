Good Data Visualization

Here is an interesting Game of Thrones visualization

Wind and Words

Wind and Words is a good visualization in that it follows Cairo's
principles of balancing beauty with good data. It collects the number of lines of one character addressing another and then draws lines between them so that the many relationships in the show are easily outlined. The site looks good without sacrificing the purpose of the data collected. The lines between characters are recognizable and easy to follow and the specific number of lines appears next to the character's image. Each season is a separate category so that the number of characters that come and go are not collected together.
