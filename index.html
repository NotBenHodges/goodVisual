Good Data Visualization

Here is an interesting Game of Thrones visualization

Wind and Words

Wind and Words is a good visualization in that it follows Cairo's
principles of balancing beauty with good data. It collects the number
of lines of one character addressing another and then draws lines
between them so that the many relationships in the show are easily
outlined. The site looks good without sacrificing the purpose of the
data collected. The lines between characters are recognizable and easy
to follow and the specific number of lines appears next to the character's
image. Each season is a separate category so that the number of characters
that come and go are not collected together.

Bad Data Visualization

Here is a visualization not as good.

Attack of the Debt Monkey

Attack of the debt monkey is a bad data visualization according to the standards of Cairo. Cairo frequently emphasizes the need for visuals that are truthful before anything else. This includes unique aesthetic choices. The graph that features a monkey on a ranger's back does very little to effectively communicate any meaningful data. We have no hard representation of the data in this image which makes it hard to read despite the unique design.
